The Mercedes-Benz W205 is the fourth generation of the Mercedes-Benz C-Class which was produced by Daimler AG between 2014 and 2021. 
The W205 C-Class was preceded by the W204 C-Class. 
The fourth-generation C-Class is available in sedan (W205), station wagon/estate (S205), coupe (C205), and cabriolet (A205) body styles.
